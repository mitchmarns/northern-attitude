Left Wings play on the left side of the ice, focusing on scoring goals and forechecking in the offensive zone.

Right Wings play on the right side of the ice, focusing on scoring goals and applying pressure in the offensive zone.

Defensemen protect their own net, block shots, deliver checks, and start offensive plays from the back.

Goalies are the last line of defense, using their skills to stop pucks from entering the net.
